We have been serving the Montgomery area for over 25 years.

Owner of Montgomery Transmission Specialist (MTS), Eddie Burley, has been working on transmissions his whole life as a family owned and operated company. Eddie's focus has always been to provide quality repairs at a fair price.

Here at MTS, we specialize in late model transmissions: including foreign and domestic, automatic, front and rear wheel drive. We provide a warranty of 18 months or at 18,000 miles and we accept most aftermarket insurances.

MTS is a member of Automatic Transmission Rebuilders Association (ATRA). We are experienced mechanics who take pride in our work and want to provide the best service possible for you. We will not take shortcuts and will always treat each car like it was our own.
